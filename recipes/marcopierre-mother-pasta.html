Marco Pierre White: Mother's Pasta

Description:

"This has got to be one of the easiest pasta dishes in the world, without a doubt one of my favorites." - Marco Pierre White

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 8 rashers of streaky bacon
  • 400g of spaghetti
  • Tomato puree
  • Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Ensure you have a pot of boiling water and a hot frying pan ready simultaneously before starting.
  2. Add dried pasta to pot of boiling water. (Approx 10 minutes)
  3. (Optional) Add a small amount of olive oil to boiling water.
  4. Add olive oil to frying pan.
  5. Add onions & streaky bacon to frying pan.
  6. Add garlic to frying pan during the final few minutes of cooking.
  7. Add tomato puree to frying pan. Mix everything together on low heat.
  8. Remove pasta from boiling water and add into frying pan mixture.
  9. Ensure everything is mixed evenly.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
